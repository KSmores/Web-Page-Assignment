Maintenance | Repair | Operations

In the military, AR collaborates with CAD data, creating a better visual so that maintaining MRO info can be more efficient. This leads to faster results and a better workforce. AR smoothens the performance service technicians use to conduct MRO tasks so that they will run more efficiently and reduce repair times while also increasing first-time fix rates. This process decreases collateral damage from failed missions that may have been caused from damaged military equipment. AR is advanced technology that the younger generation has been able to obtain more easily and efficiently. The use of AR in the military enables inexperienced MRO workers to work at a fast pace more efficiently. Which leads to numerous MRO workers in the field that improve military assets for the future.

Training

AR is also used in military training by setting targets as well as tracking soldiers in relations to set targets.
