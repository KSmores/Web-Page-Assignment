How AR is incorporated in our Daily lives

AR is used quite often in our daily lives. Three common uses are:

  1. Sportsvision

    2. In 1998, the NFL broadcasted it's first live game with the yellow yard marker that determined the 1st and 10 graphic system.



  2. Shopping

    3. A few years ago, IKEA released an app called IKEA Place that shows how furniture or items purchased online would look and fit in a designated area. This would be great for moving or just rearranging specifically now during quarantine.



  3. Snapchat

    4. Snapchat AR Lenses transforms the way you look as well as background affects by using 3D effects, objects, characters, and transformations.For example, the forever trend of the dog filter, adding locations, bitmoji's and etc. It's all powered by AR Lenses.
